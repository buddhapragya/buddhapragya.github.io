Welcome to my homepage!

I am a Buddhist monk ordained in the Korean Chogye Order - Myanmar Theravada tradition. I practice meditation according to the Korean Zen tradition which is rooted in Indian and Chinese Zen Buddhism. I combine it with mindfulness practice as taught in Theravada Buddhism.

Before becoming a monk, I worked as a theoretical computer scientist.
