Welcome to my homepage!

I am a Buddhist monk ordained in the Korean Jogye Order - Myanmar Theravada tradition. I practice meditation according to the Korean Zen tradition which is rooted in Indian Zen (Dhyana) and Chinese Zen (Chan) Buddhism. I combine it with mindfulness practice and other teachings of Theravada Buddhism.

After undergoing monastic training in Korea for around four years, I moved to India where I am currently living.

Before becoming a monk, I worked as a theoretical computer scientist. During that period I was a part-time meditator in the European Zen Centers of the international Kwan Um School of Zen. My experience there has provided the direction for my meditation practice till now. I learnt a lot from Zen Master Wu Bong (Jacob Perl), Zen Master Bon Yo (Grazyna Perl) and several other teachers.
