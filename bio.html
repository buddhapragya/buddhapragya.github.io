Kumar Neeraj Verma

Bio-sketch













1979 born in India
1996-2000 BTech, department of computer science and engineering, IIT Delhi
2000-2003 PhD in computer science, ENS Paris-Saclay
2003-2009 computer scientist at Institut fuer Informatik, Technische Universitaet Muenchen, Germany
2009 became postulant Buddhist monk at Mu Sang Sa monastery, South Korea
2011 became novice Buddhist monk in the Jogye Order of Korean Buddhism
2013 became fully ordained Buddhist monk in India in the Myanmar Buddhist tradition. Received name Buddhapragya (बुद्धप्रज्ञा)
